# Re-Engagement Email Sequence

> 3-email sequence for cold leads (30+ days of no contact). Use Prompt Chain #7 from `prompts/follow-up-prompts.md` to batch-personalize these for multiple leads at once.

---

## Email 1: The "Thought of You" (Day 1)

**Subject:** [First Name], this made me think of you

---

Hi [First Name],

It's been a while since we last connected about [what they originally inquired about], and I wanted to reach out because something reminded me of you.

[Choose one of these approaches — whichever fits best:]

**Option A (News/Trend):**
I just read that [relevant industry trend or news]. Given what you told me about [their situation], I thought you'd find this interesting: [1–2 sentence insight about what this means for them].

**Option B (Client Result):**
We recently helped someone in a similar situation to yours — [brief result]. It made me think about our earlier conversation and whether you're still dealing with [their problem].

**Option C (New Offering):**
Since we last talked, we've [developed something new / refined our approach / gotten results with a new method] that's directly relevant to [their problem].

No pressure at all — just wanted to reconnect and see how things are going.

[Your Name]

---

## Email 2: The Value Drop (Day 5)

**Subject:** Free [resource type] for [their problem]

---

Hi [First Name],

Following up on my last note — I put together [a guide / checklist / framework / template] that addresses [their specific problem].

**Here it is:** [Link or inline content]

It covers:
- [Useful thing 1]
- [Useful thing 2]
- [Useful thing 3]

It's yours whether or not we ever work together. I just want it to be useful.

If it sparks any questions or you want to talk through how it applies to [their business], I'm here.

[Your Name]

---

## Email 3: The Direct Ask (Day 10)

**Subject:** Quick question about [their project/goal]

---

Hi [First Name],

I'll keep this short — I've reached out a couple times and I know you're busy.

Three quick questions (reply with just the number if you want):

1. **Still interested** — yes, let's find time to talk about [their problem]
2. **Not right now** — check back with me in [they can fill in a timeframe]
3. **Not a fit** — please take me off your list (no hard feelings!)

Whatever the answer, I appreciate your honesty. It helps me focus my time on people I can actually help right now.

[Your Name]

P.S. If you reply "1," I'll send over a couple of time options for a quick call. No prep needed on your end.

---

## Sequence Notes

**When to use this:** For any lead that went cold 30+ days ago. Run Prompt Chain #7 monthly to batch-generate personalized versions for all your cold leads at once.

**Response handling:**
- **Reply "1":** Send calendar link immediately. Use Prompt Chain #6 (Response Handler) for the reply.
- **Reply "2":** Add a follow-up reminder for the date they suggest. Send a brief "Sounds good, I'll check back in [timeframe]" reply.
- **Reply "3":** Remove from active follow-up. Send a gracious response: "Totally understand. If anything changes, my door is always open."
- **No reply after Email 3:** Move to "inactive" in your tracker. Try again in 90 days with a completely fresh approach.

**Success rate:** Expect 10–20% response rate on re-engagement sequences. Even a 10% response rate on 20 cold leads = 2 conversations = potential revenue you were leaving on the table.
