# Welcome Email Sequence

> 5-email sequence triggered when a new client signs. Customize the [bracketed sections]. Use Prompt Chain #2 from `prompts/onboarding-prompts.md` to auto-generate a personalized version.

---

## Email 1: Welcome (Send Immediately)

**Subject:** Welcome to [Your Company] — here's what happens next

---

Hi [Client First Name],

Welcome aboard! We're excited to work with you on [project/service name].

Here's exactly what happens next (so you're never wondering "what's going on?"):

**Step 1 (Today):** Complete your intake form
It takes about 10 minutes and helps us start strong on Day 1 — no back-and-forth needed.

**[Link to Intake Form]**

**Step 2 (Within 48 hours):** We'll send your kickoff call agenda
Based on your intake form answers, we'll create a customized agenda so our first call is focused and productive.

**Step 3 (This week):** Kickoff call
We'll align on everything and start work immediately.

**One important thing:** The faster you complete the intake form, the faster we can get started. Most clients finish it in one sitting.

Talk soon,
[Your Name]

P.S. If you have any questions before the kickoff call, just reply to this email. We typically respond within a few hours.

---

## Email 2: How We Work Together (Send Day 1)

**Subject:** How we'll work together (our process)

---

Hi [Client First Name],

Now that you're officially on board, here's how we work together so there are no surprises:

**Communication:**
- Primary channel: [Email / Slack / Other]
- Response time: We respond within [X hours] on business days
- Urgent items: [Phone/text] for anything truly time-sensitive

**Deliverables:**
- You'll receive [deliverable type] every [frequency]
- Each deliverable includes a brief summary of what changed and why
- Feedback turnaround: We ask for your input within [X days] to keep the project on track

**Meetings:**
- [Weekly/biweekly] check-in calls ([X] minutes)
- Agenda sent 24 hours before each call
- Summary + action items sent within 24 hours after

**What we need from you:**
- Timely feedback on deliverables (within [X] business days)
- Access to [tools/platforms] we discussed
- A quick reply when we have questions (keeps everything moving)

**What you can expect from us:**
- Proactive communication (we'll tell you about problems before they become problems)
- On-time delivery (if a timeline shifts, you'll know immediately and why)
- Honest recommendations (even when they're not what you want to hear)

Questions about any of this? Just reply.

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## Email 3: Access & Resources (Send Day 2)

**Subject:** Quick setup items (5 minutes)

---

Hi [Client First Name],

Before our kickoff call, we need a few things set up. This takes about 5 minutes:

**Please share access to:** (use the secure link below — never send passwords via email)
- [ ] [Platform 1] — [why we need it]
- [ ] [Platform 2] — [why we need it]
- [ ] [Any files or documents we need]

**Secure sharing link:** [Link to your preferred secure sharing method]

**We've already set up:**
- [x] Your project folder: [Link]
- [x] Your communication channel: [Link]
- [x] Your project timeline: [Link]

If you're not sure about any of these, no worries — we'll sort it out on the kickoff call.

Thanks,
[Your Name]

---

## Email 4: Kickoff Call Prep (Send Day 3)

**Subject:** Your kickoff call agenda (please read before we meet)

---

Hi [Client First Name],

Our kickoff call is scheduled for **[Date] at [Time]**. Here's the agenda so we make the most of our time:

**Kickoff Agenda ([30/60] minutes):**

1. **Quick intros** (5 min) — Who's who and what everyone's role is
2. **Your goals recap** (10 min) — Confirm what success looks like based on your intake form
3. **Our approach** (10 min) — Walk through the plan, timeline, and milestones
4. **Questions & concerns** (5 min) — Anything on your mind before we start
5. **Next steps & first deliverable preview** (5 min) — What happens after this call

**Before the call, please:**
- [ ] Review the agenda above
- [ ] Complete the intake form if you haven't yet ([Link])
- [ ] Write down your top 2–3 questions or concerns
- [ ] Have [any relevant documents/data] handy

**Meeting link:** [Link]

See you [Day]!
[Your Name]

---
