## Monthly Content Themes

Use one theme per week to maintain coherence. Rotate through these 4 categories:

| Week | Theme | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| Week 1 | **Authority** | Share expertise, frameworks, and original thinking |
| Week 2 | **Proof** | Client results, case studies, testimonials, before/after |
| Week 3 | **Connection** | Personal stories, behind-the-scenes, values, opinions |
| Week 4 | **Offer** | Direct CTA, service spotlight, limited availability, launch |

## Quick Reference: Content Types That Convert

**Highest engagement (in order):**
1. Personal story with a business lesson
2. Controversial/hot take with reasoning
3. Step-by-step tactical how-to
4. Client result with specific numbers
5. "Mistake I made" vulnerability post

**Lowest engagement (avoid):**
1. Generic motivational quotes
2. "I'm excited to announce..." posts
3. Links with no context
4. Reposts without commentary
5. Long paragraphs with no formatting
