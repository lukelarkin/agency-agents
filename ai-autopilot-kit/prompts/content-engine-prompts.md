## Prompt Chain #1: Business Voice Extraction

> **Used in:** System 1, Step 1 (One-time setup)
> **Purpose:** Creates your "Content DNA" document that makes all AI output sound like YOU

### Prompt 1A: Voice Analysis

```
I need you to analyze my writing voice so you can replicate it in future content. Below are 3 examples of content I've written that feel authentically "me."

Read all 3 carefully, then create a detailed "Content DNA" profile that captures:

1. **Tone & Personality:** Am I formal or casual? Sarcastic or earnest? High-energy or calm? Authoritative or conversational?
2. **Sentence Structure:** Do I use short punchy sentences or long flowing ones? Do I use fragments? How do I transition between ideas?
3. **Vocabulary Patterns:** What kinds of words do I gravitate toward? Do I use jargon? Slang? Metaphors? What's my reading level?
4. **Signature Phrases:** Any recurring phrases, verbal tics, or stylistic quirks?
5. **Emotional Range:** What emotions do I express? How do I handle serious vs. light topics?
6. **Point of View:** How do I position myself relative to the reader? Teacher? Peer? Mentor? Friend?
7. **Formatting Habits:** How do I use paragraphs, lists, bold, italics, headers?

**Example 1:**
[PASTE YOUR FIRST CONTENT EXAMPLE HERE]

**Example 2:**
[PASTE YOUR SECOND CONTENT EXAMPLE HERE]

**Example 3:**
[PASTE YOUR THIRD CONTENT EXAMPLE HERE]
```

### Prompt 1B: Content DNA Finalization

```
Great analysis. Now combine that with my business context to create my final Content DNA document.

**My Business:**
- I help [YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE] with [YOUR SERVICE/OFFER]
- My top 5 topics/themes: [TOPIC 1], [TOPIC 2], [TOPIC 3], [TOPIC 4], [TOPIC 5]
- My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CLIENT — role, industry, pain points, goals]
- My unique angle: [WHAT MAKES YOUR PERSPECTIVE DIFFERENT FROM OTHERS IN YOUR SPACE]
- Words/phrases I NEVER use: [ANY WORDS THAT FEEL OFF-BRAND]

Create a complete Content DNA document I can paste into any future prompt to ensure the AI writes in my voice. Format it as a reference card I can copy-paste.
```

## Prompt Chain #2: Weekly Content Seed Generator

> **Used in:** System 1, Step 2 (Every Monday)
> **Purpose:** Turns one idea into a week's worth of content angles

```
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT DNA HERE]

---

I need to create a full week of content from a single idea. Here's my content seed:

**This week's seed:** [ONE SENTENCE ABOUT SOMETHING THAT HAPPENED IN YOUR BUSINESS — a client win, a lesson learned, an observation, a mistake, a conversation, an industry trend]

Turn this into a Content Seed Document with:

1. **Core Message:** The single big idea in one sentence
2. **7 Angles:** 7 different ways to approach this topic (list each with a one-line description)
   - A personal story angle
   - A tactical how-to angle
   - A contrarian/hot take angle
   - A "mistake I made" angle
   - A data/proof angle
   - A beginner-friendly angle
   - A behind-the-scenes angle
3. **10 Hooks:** 10 opening lines that would stop someone from scrolling (first lines only)
4. **Key Talking Points:** 5 specific points, examples, or data I can reference across multiple pieces
5. **CTA Ideas:** 3 calls-to-action that connect this topic to my offer
6. **Hashtags/Keywords:** 5–7 relevant hashtags and 2–3 SEO keywords
```

## Prompt Chain #3: Social Post Batch Creator

> **Used in:** System 1, Step 3 (Every Monday)
> **Purpose:** Creates 5 platform-ready social media posts

```
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT DNA HERE]

---

Using the content seed below, create 5 social media posts:

**Content Seed:** [PASTE YOUR CONTENT SEED FROM CHAIN #2, OR SUMMARIZE THE KEY POINTS]

Create these 5 posts:

**Post 1 — LinkedIn (Story-Based):**
- Hook that stops the scroll (first line is everything)
- Personal story or client story that illustrates the point
- The lesson or insight
- Soft CTA (comment, share, or DM)
- 800–1,200 characters
- Use line breaks for readability

**Post 2 — LinkedIn (Tactical):**
- Hook with a specific promise ("How to X in Y steps")
- Numbered steps or framework
- Each step is 1–2 sentences max
- End with a question to drive comments
- 600–1,000 characters

**Post 3 — Instagram/Facebook (Carousel Concept):**
- Slide 1: Bold hook/title (8 words max)
- Slides 2–6: One key point per slide (headline + 1-sentence explanation)
- Slide 7: Summary + CTA
- Also write the caption (150–200 words with hashtags)

**Post 4 — Instagram/Facebook (Short-Form):**
- 3–5 sentence post
- Punchy, quotable, shareable
- Designed to get saves and shares
- Include relevant hashtags

**Post 5 — Twitter/X Thread (5–7 tweets):**
- Tweet 1: Hook (the "I need to read this" moment)
- Tweets 2–6: One idea per tweet, building on each other
- Final tweet: Summary + CTA + "Follow for more [topic]"
- Each tweet under 280 characters

Write in my voice (see Content DNA above). No generic AI-speak. Every post should feel like I actually wrote it.
```

## Prompt Chain #4: Email Newsletter Generator

> **Used in:** System 1, Step 4 (Every Monday)
> **Purpose:** Creates your weekly email newsletter

```
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT DNA HERE]

---

Write my weekly email newsletter using this content seed and context:

**Content Seed:** [PASTE SEED OR KEY POINTS]
**Audience:** [YOUR EMAIL LIST DESCRIPTION — who are they, what do they expect from your emails]
**My offer/CTA this week:** [WHAT DO YOU WANT READERS TO DO — book a call, check out a resource, reply, etc.]

Create:

**Subject Lines (3 options, ranked):**
Rank by open-rate potential. Use curiosity, specificity, or urgency. No clickbait.

**Email Body:**
Format: Story → Lesson → CTA

- **Opening hook** (2–3 sentences): Pull them in with a story, question, or surprising statement
- **The story/context** (3–5 sentences): Set the scene. What happened?
- **The lesson/insight** (3–5 sentences): What's the takeaway? Make it actionable.
- **The bridge to CTA** (1–2 sentences): Connect the lesson to what you're offering
- **CTA** (clear, specific, one action): Tell them exactly what to do next
- **P.S. line**: Secondary CTA or personal note (these get read more than you'd think)

Keep the whole email under 500 words. Write it like an email to a smart friend, not a marketing blast.
```

## Prompt Chain #5: Blog Post Generator

> **Used in:** System 1, Step 5 (Every Monday)
> **Purpose:** Creates an SEO-optimized blog post

```
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT DNA HERE]

---

Write a blog post based on this week's content seed and email:

**Content Seed:** [PASTE SEED OR KEY POINTS]
**Email Newsletter:** [PASTE THE EMAIL YOU JUST CREATED — the blog should expand on it]
**Target keyword:** [YOUR SEO KEYWORD — or say "suggest one based on the topic"]
**My audience's search intent:** [WHAT WOULD SOMEONE GOOGLE TO FIND THIS POST?]

Create:

1. **Headline** (SEO-optimized, under 60 characters, includes keyword)
2. **Meta description** (under 155 characters, compelling click-driver)
3. **Blog post** (800–1,200 words):
   - Opening hook (don't start with "In today's..." — start with a story, question, or bold statement)
   - The problem (why the reader should care)
   - Your take/framework/solution (the meat of the post)
   - Actionable steps (what they can do right now)
   - Conclusion with CTA
4. **3 internal linking suggestions** (topics I should link to from this post)
5. **Social snippet** (a quotable excerpt I can share to promote the post)

Write in my voice. Optimize for readability: short paragraphs, subheadings every 2–3 paragraphs, bold key phrases.
```

## Prompt Chain #10: LinkedIn Article Writer

> **Purpose:** Creates long-form LinkedIn articles (different from regular posts)

```
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT DNA HERE]

---

Write a LinkedIn article on:

**Topic:** [YOUR TOPIC]
**Key argument:** [YOUR MAIN POINT OR THESIS]
**Target reader:** [WHO SPECIFICALLY SHOULD READ THIS]
**Length:** 1,000–1,500 words

Structure:
1. **Headline:** Specific and compelling (not generic)
2. **Opening:** Start with a story, surprising stat, or bold claim — not "In my experience..."
3. **The problem:** Why this topic matters right now
4. **Your framework/approach:** The core of the article (3–5 key points)
5. **Evidence:** Examples, data, stories for each point
6. **Actionable takeaway:** What the reader can do today
7. **Closing:** Strong ending + CTA (follow me, comment, visit my site)

```

## Prompt Chain #15: Video Script Writer

> **Purpose:** Creates scripts for YouTube, Instagram Reels, or TikTok

```
[PASTE YOUR CONTENT DNA HERE]

---

Write a video script for:

**Platform:** [YOUTUBE / INSTAGRAM REELS / TIKTOK]
**Topic:** [YOUR TOPIC]
**Length:** [SHORT: 30-60 sec / MEDIUM: 2-5 min / LONG: 8-15 min]
**Goal:** [EDUCATE / ENTERTAIN / CONVERT]

Create:

**For short-form (Reels/TikTok):**
- Hook (first 3 seconds — the most important part)
- Body (the content — fast-paced, one idea)
- CTA (follow, comment, or check link in bio)
- Caption + hashtags
- On-screen text overlay suggestions

**For long-form (YouTube):**
- Title (SEO-optimized, clickable)
- Thumbnail concept (what image + text)
- Hook (first 30 seconds — why they should keep watching)
- Intro (brief, establish credibility)
- Main content (structured with timestamps)
- CTA (subscribe, comment, check description)
- Description with keywords

Write it as a spoken script — the way I'd actually say it, not the way I'd write it. Include [PAUSE], [EMPHASIS], and [CUT TO] markers where helpful.
```
