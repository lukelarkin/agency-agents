# The AI Tool Stack Guide

> The exact tools we recommend for running your AI Autopilot systems. Organized by budget level so you can start free and upgrade as you grow.

---

## The Essential Stack (What You Actually Need)

You need exactly **3 things** to run every system in this kit:

1. **An AI chat tool** (for running the prompt chains)
2. **A writing/document tool** (for saving outputs)
3. **A scheduling/tracking tool** (for managing your calendar and leads)

That's it. Everything else is optional optimization.

---

## Tier 1: Free Stack ($0/month)

Best for: Just getting started, testing the systems, budget-conscious

| Purpose | Tool | Why This One |
|---------|------|-------------|
| AI Chat | **ChatGPT Free** or **Claude.ai Free** | Both work great for the prompt chains. Claude tends to write more naturally; ChatGPT is more widely available |
| Documents | **Google Docs** | Free, shareable, works everywhere |
| Email | **Gmail** | Draft templates for your sequences |
| Scheduling | **Google Calendar** | Time-block your content creation and follow-ups |
| Lead Tracking | **Google Sheets** | Simple spreadsheet tracker (Prompt Chain #1 from System 5 builds this for you) |
| Social Scheduling | **Native platform scheduling** | LinkedIn, Facebook, and X all have built-in schedulers |
| Forms | **Google Forms** | For your client intake form |

**Total: $0/month**

## Tier 2: Growth Stack ($50–$100/month)

Best for: Running all 5 systems consistently, 3+ clients/month

| Purpose | Tool | Cost | Why Upgrade |
|---------|------|------|-------------|
| AI Chat | **Claude Pro** or **ChatGPT Plus** | $20/mo | Longer conversations, better outputs, faster responses |
| Documents | **Notion** | Free–$10/mo | Central hub for all your systems, templates, and client info |
| Email Marketing | **ConvertKit** or **Mailchimp** | $0–$29/mo | Automate your welcome sequences and newsletters |
| Social Scheduling | **Buffer** | $6/mo | Schedule across all platforms from one dashboard |
| Lead Tracking | **Notion** or **Airtable** | Free–$10/mo | More structured than a spreadsheet, easier to maintain |
| Proposals | **Google Docs** (free) or **Canva Docs** | Free | Formatted proposals that look professional |
| Calendar/Booking | **Calendly** (free tier) | $0 | Clients self-schedule kickoff calls and meetings |

**Total: ~$50–$75/month**

| Purpose | Tool | Cost | Why Upgrade |

## Our Recommendation

**If you're just starting:** Use the Free Stack for 30 days. Prove the systems work before spending money on tools.

**If you're ready to invest:** Start with Claude Pro ($20/mo) + Buffer ($6/mo) + Calendly (free). That covers 90% of what you need for $26/month.

**If you're scaling:** Add a CRM (HubSpot free tier) and ConvertKit ($29/mo) when you're consistently getting 5+ leads/month.

> The tools don't make the system — the prompts and processes do. A free stack running consistently beats a $300/month stack used sporadically.
