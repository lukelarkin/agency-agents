# 30-Day Implementation Plan

> Don't try to set up all 5 systems at once. Follow this day-by-day plan to have everything running smoothly in 30 days with minimal overwhelm.

---

## The Rule: One System Per Week

- **Week 1:** Content Engine (highest immediate impact)
- **Week 2:** Client Onboarding (next client gets the premium experience)
- **Week 3:** Proposal Generator (start closing deals faster)
- **Week 4:** Lead Follow-Up + Weekly Review (revenue machine + CEO clarity)

**Daily time commitment:** 30–60 minutes per day. That's it.

---

## Week 1: The Content Engine (Days 1–7)

### Day 1: Foundation (45 min)
- [ ] Read `systems/01-content-engine.md` (10 min)
- [ ] Gather your 3 best content examples + audience description (15 min)
- [ ] Run Prompt Chain #1: Business Voice Extraction (20 min)
- [ ] Save your Content DNA document

### Day 2: First Content Batch (60 min)
- [ ] Think of one thing that happened in your business this week (5 min)
- [ ] Run Prompt Chain #2: Weekly Content Seed Generator (10 min)
- [ ] Run Prompt Chain #3: Social Post Batch Creator (15 min)
- [ ] Run Prompt Chain #4: Email Newsletter Generator (15 min)
- [ ] Run Prompt Chain #5: Blog Post Generator (15 min)

### Day 3: Edit & Schedule (30 min)
- [ ] Review and lightly edit all content from yesterday (20 min)
- [ ] Set up content calendar using `templates/content-calendar-template.md` (10 min)

### Day 4: Schedule & Publish (30 min)
- [ ] Schedule social posts for the week (15 min)
- [ ] Schedule email newsletter (10 min)
- [ ] Publish or schedule blog post (5 min)

### Day 5: Review & Refine (30 min)
- [ ] Check early engagement on published content (10 min)
- [ ] Run Prompt Chain #6: Content Performance Reviewer (if you have data) (10 min)
- [ ] Refine your Content DNA based on what felt right/wrong (10 min)

### Days 6–7: Rest & Observe
- [ ] Monitor engagement on your content
- [ ] Note what got the most response — you'll use this next week
- [ ] Celebrate: you just created a week of content in ~3 hours total

**Week 1 milestone:** You have a Content DNA document and one full week of content created, scheduled, and published.

## Troubleshooting Common Stalls

**"I got stuck on Day 2 and haven't gone back."**
Skip ahead. The systems are independent — you can start with whichever one solves your biggest pain point right now.

**"The AI output doesn't sound like me."**
Re-run Prompt Chain #1 (Business Voice Extraction) with better examples. The Content DNA quality determines everything downstream.

**"I don't have any leads to follow up with."**
Start with System 1 (Content Engine) and System 3 (Proposals). Follow-up comes after you have people to follow up with.

**"This feels like too much."**
Pick ONE system. Run it for 2 weeks. Once it's a habit, add the next one. Slow and consistent beats fast and abandoned.
